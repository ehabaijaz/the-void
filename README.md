##  About ⬛
- The Void is an interdimensional collection of small projects, sometimes disconnected, sometimes confusing but all lead to each other one way or another.
- This project contains 3/4 of the games The Void presents. The final Metroidvania is in a different project due to it's scope.
- Play 3 projects created in Godot, all with a unique art style.
  
## Games ⚫
- Main Menu: Access these games through a Main Menu.
- Rocket Launch: Press a button and a rocket launches. Press it again and it launches again.
- Piggy: Control a pig and collect apples to grow bigger. Oddly satisfying.
- Space Shooter: A comparable leap in quality. Control the rocket ship you launched in space shooting monsters and increase your score. 
- Metroidvania: IN a different project but coming soon i promise 

## Controls ▪️
- Main Menu: Space Bar to play the Space Shooter. Click on the buttons in the bottom left and bottom right of the screen to play Rocket Launch and Piggy.
- Rocket Launch: Click on the 'Launch' button to launch the ship. Click it again to launch it again.. and again... and again..
- Piggy: Use arrow keys or WASD to control the pig and it automatically collects the apples to grow in size.
- Space Shooter: Use up and down keys to move the spaceship and spacebar or enter key to shoot projectiles. Don't get hit by the enemies.
- Metroidvania: Soon™ 

No AI was used in this game(s), nor in this README or itch description.
